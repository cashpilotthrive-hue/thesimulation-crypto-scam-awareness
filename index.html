How It Works

Our step-by-step recovery process

1

Submit Report

Fill out our online form with all available details about the crime. The more information, the better.

2

Case Assignment

Within 24 hours, you'll be assigned a dedicated case manager who will guide you through the process.

3

Investigation

Our forensic team analyzes blockchain transactions, traces wallets, and coordinates with exchanges.

4

Recovery Action

We work with law enforcement and financial institutions to freeze and recover your assets when possible.

5

Resolution

You receive a final report with recovery results and recommendations for future protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from victims

How long does the recovery process take?

Recovery timelines vary depending on case complexity. Simple cases may resolve in weeks, while complex international cases can take months. You'll receive regular updates from your case manager.

Is there a fee for your services?

Our initial consultation and report filing are free. Recovery services may involve legal fees which will be discussed upfront. We work on a contingency basis for successful recoveries.

Can you guarantee recovery of my funds?

While we have a high success rate, we cannot guarantee recovery in all cases. Factors include how quickly you report, the complexity of the crime, and whether the funds have been moved through mixers.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll need details about the incident including dates, amounts, wallet addresses, transaction hashes, communications with scammers, and any evidence you have. The more detail, the better.

Do you handle international cases?

Yes! We work with international law enforcement and have partnerships with agencies worldwide. Cryptocurrency crime is inherently cross-border.

How do I know this is legitimate?

CCRC is a registered government agency. You can verify our credentials through official government directories. We never ask for payment upfront via wire transfer or gift cards.
