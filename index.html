Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for cryptocurrency crime victims

🔍

Blockchain Forensics

Our expert investigators trace stolen funds across blockchain networks using advanced forensic tools.

  • Transaction tracing
  • Wallet identification
  • Exchange coordination
  • Evidence preservation
💰

Asset Recovery

We work with exchanges and law enforcement worldwide to freeze and recover stolen cryptocurrency.

  • Freeze requests
  • Legal coordination
  • Asset seizure orders
  • Restitution assistance

Wallet Security

How It Works

Our streamlined process to help you recover your assets

1

Submit Report

File a detailed report with all transaction details, wallet addresses, and evidence of the crime.

2

Case Assignment

Your case is assigned to a dedicated investigator who specializes in your type of cryptocurrency crime.

3

Investigation

We conduct blockchain analysis, coordinate with exchanges, and work with law enforcement.

4

Recovery Action

Funds are traced, accounts frozen, and recovery procedures initiated where possible.

5

Resolution

You receive a final report with outcomes and restitution guidance if recovery is successful.
