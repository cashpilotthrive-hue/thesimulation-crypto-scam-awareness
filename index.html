Our Professional Services

Comprehensive solutions for all types of cryptocurrency crimes

💰

Investment Scam Recovery

Recover funds lost to fake investment platforms, Ponzi schemes, and fraudulent crypto projects.

  • Blockchain forensics analysis
  • Wallet tracing & identification
  • Legal coordination
  • Exchange cooperation
🔓

Cryptocurrency Hack Investigation

Professional investigation of exchange hacks, DeFi exploits, and smart contract vulnerabilities.

  • Technical incident analysis
  • Exploit vector identification
  • Fund tracking across chains
  • Law enforcement liaison
👤

Stolen Crypto Recovery

Track and recover stolen digital assets from phishing, rug pulls, and unauthorized access.

  • Real-time blockchain monitoring
  • Wallet freeze requests
  • Cross-chain tracing
  • Recovery negotiation
🎣

Phishing Attack Support

Immediate assistance for phishing victims to secure remaining assets and trace attackers.

  • Emergency asset securing
  • Attack vector analysis
  • Social engineering investigation
  • Prevention guidance
🏦

Exchange Fraud

  • Platform investigation
  • Regulatory coordination
  • Asset tracing
  • Class action support

Legal & Advisory
