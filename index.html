Our Services

Comprehensive support for cryptocurrency crime victims

🔍

Blockchain Investigation

Our expert investigators trace stolen funds across blockchain networks using advanced forensic tools.

  • Transaction tracing
  • Wallet identification
  • Exchange coordination
  • Evidence collection
💰

Asset Recovery

Working with international law enforcement to freeze and recover stolen cryptocurrency assets.

  • Asset freezing orders
  • Legal coordination
  • Exchange liaison
  • Recovery negotiation
📊

Case Management

Dedicated case managers guide you through the recovery process with regular updates.

  • Personal case manager
  • Regular progress updates
  • Document coordination
  • Emotional support

Legal Support

  • Legal referrals
  • Law enforcement coordination
  • International cooperation
  • Court testimony

Security Assessment

How the Recovery Process Works

1

Submit Report

2

Case Assignment

Your case is assigned to a dedicated investigator within 24 hours.

3

Investigation

We trace transactions and coordinate with exchanges.

4

Recovery Action

We work with law enforcement to freeze and recover assets.

5

Resolution

You receive a final report with outcomes.
